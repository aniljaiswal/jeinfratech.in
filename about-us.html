Who We Are

Founded in May, 2013 in New Delhi by Prem Jaiswal & Sudhir Jaiswal, JE INFRATECH is a company specializing in Large Scale Residential & Commerical Construction, Interior Design & Decoration and is committed to delivering Design Excellence. We are a Design & Build Company. It was the constant motivation and feedback of customers of Jaiswal Electric Works (Parent Firm) which led us to open a new venture and provide construction & design related serives.

About Jaiswal Electric Works

Founded in 1982 by Prem Jaiswal, the company has more than 30 years of experience in industrial as well as residential electrical projects and has successfully executed 150+ contracts. It also provides all kinds of Electrical supplies ranging from cables, switches to Distribution Boxes and handles our electrical contracts.

Quality Policy

We are constantly working to improve our quality of service to our customers and want to hear feedback from our stakeholders. In order to maintain our continuing high standards we issue a customer satisfaction report on all completed rojects that our customers may complete and return. This way we constantly monitor the quality of workmanship through to the quality of management.
