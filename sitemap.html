Who We Are

Founded in May, 2013 in New Delhi by Prem Jaiswal & Sudhir Jaiswal, JE INFRATECH is a company specializing in Large Scale Residential & Commerical Construction, Interior Design & Decoration and is committed to delivering Design Excellence. We are a Design & Build Company. It was the constant motivation and feedback of customers of Jaiswal Electric Works (Parent Firm) which led us to open a new venture and provide construction & design related serives.
