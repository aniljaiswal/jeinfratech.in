Civil Construction

Our in-depth understanding of the construction industry, combined with labour management expertise, has enabled us to successfully render Civil Construction Services to a variety of clients. We follow stringent norms to offer best quality of services at the most reasonable price.

Bringing the latest in technology and equipment to complete your construction projects, our goal is to not only help you design and build your dream project, but to make the process easy and enjoyable for you. We are a full-service design / build company with a wide range of experience.

Some of the unique features of our construction services:

  • Construction Management

    As your Construction Manager, JE INFRATECH will act as your agent throughout the design and construction phases of your building project, until the day when you turn on the lights in your new or renovated building for the very first time.

  • Cost Estimation

    Accurate and well-defined cost estimates are critical to the success of every project. Our estimators specialize in providing accurate construction cost estimates from the initial programming phase through the detailed design and construction phases. Our cost estimating procedures are designed to ensure that each element of the project is well defined and accounted for in the estimate format.

  • Scheduling

    During the design phase of a project, critical baseline decisions relating to budget, form, and function are made. As the design continues to develop, so does the detailed master construction schedule. This schedule, which highlights activity periods for major subcontracts and material deliveries, may be included in the bid documents as a guide to the Contractors when establishing their proposals.

  • Facility Assessments

    It is sometimes necessary to evaluate the condition and life expectancy of an existing facility. Whether as condition of sale or to determine the value of the facility for consideration of future renovations or additions, JE INFRATECH can assist with this important service. Our staff of professionals can help evaluate the condition of these elements of an existing facility such as structural systems, exterior wall and roof systems and mechanical and electrical systems now or in the future.

Aluminum Works

We are widely appreciated for the supply of an exclusive gamut of aluminum work that includes- glazing structure, aluminium curtain wall and aluminum cladding. These aluminium works are designed to perfection and reflect the innovative designs and craftsmanship of the designers and engineers.

The company offers a wide range of products options in the following categories :

  • Structural Glazing

    Structural glazing service we provide is a system where glazing is bonded to a metal structure. Such technique is used to create 'all glass' or 'curtain wall' facades. Some of variations of structural glazing offered by us include :

    • Single glazing (annealed, toughened, enameled)

    • Laminated glazing

    • Double glazing

  • Aluminium Cladding

    We specialize in supplying and installing aluminum composite panel cladding and other specified cladding materials. The work is done for all kind of internal as well as external applications. We provide services for :

    • Wall cladding

    • Facades and roof edging

    • Commercial interior decoration

    • Signs

    • Tunnel linings

    • Displays and exhibition stands

  • Curtain Walls

    We carefully design curtain walls in a way that they have maximum resistance towards air and water infiltration, seismic forces as well as it's own load force. Some of the options available in this category include :

    • Design options

    • Low to high rise curtain walling

    • Structural sealant glazing

    • BG beaded glazing

    • Sloped and faceted facades

    • Atrium roofs ribbon glazing concealed vents

  • Aluminium Sliding Window

    The aluminum sliding windows we offer feature top quality wheels with brass axles and an adjustable carriage. Made from premium quality aluminum, each frame of the window is tested for it's strength and extreme resistance towards heat & corrosion before it is sent at client's end. We also provide customized designing and manufacturing options in this category to customers.

  • Door Partition

    The wide range of door partition we offer is made by using aluminum frames with glass panels inside it. Such kind of partitions are usually used in office space. Available in variety of sizes and designs, we can also manufacture the partition walls as per the specifications given by the clients.

  • False Ceiling

    We, at JE INFRATECH supply and fabricate Metal false ceilings, mineral fiber ceiling, Grid ceiling, gypsum board or aluminum composite panels ceiling Our false ceiling is of extensive variety and quality, and manufactured with utmost care to fulfill the market demands. The quality architectural false ceilings manufactured and supplied by us add exquisite look to your homes and offices. Our false ceilings will make your ordinary ceilings look extraordinary. Our unique false ceiling items can transform the look of your interiors – be it office or home.

  • Entrance Door

    An array of entrance doors we offer are suitable for multi-purpose applications. These are designed and polished to give a smart and chick look to the interiors of a place. Durable in nature as well as easy to clean and maintain, these doors are available in different sizes as well as customized specifications to clients.
